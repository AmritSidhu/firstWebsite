Welcome to my Battlehips Game!

What is it?

This is a simple battleships game that runs in the terminal. Player begins the game by typing out the letter and number position from the grid (i.e. “A9”), and the game responds by determining if the called shot was a “hit” or a “miss.” Hits occur when the called shot hits any part of an enemy vessel. A miss occurs when the shot misses all enemy vessels.

  • Much better understanding of ruby fundementals, ie loops, arrays, hashes etc
